Features

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

Four tools. One focused app. Built around the exact workflow of a cleaning business owner.

Client Vault

Store everything about each client — address, contact info, gate codes, pet details, and cleaning preferences. Find anyone in 2 seconds.

Service Catalog

Define your services once — flat rate or hourly. Use them consistently across every quote so your pricing stays professional and on-brand.

Smart Quotes

Generate a professional quote in under a minute. Select client, add services, apply tax — then email it directly or convert to a job instantly.

Job Scheduling

Schedule jobs with date and time, track status from scheduled to complete, and keep your entire day organized at a glance. No more double-bookings.

How It Works

From phone call to paid job
in four simple steps.

1

Add the client

Store their name, address, contact info, gate codes, and any special notes. Takes 60 seconds. Find them instantly next time.

2

Select the client, pick services from your catalog, add tax if needed, and send the professional quote straight to their inbox — all from one screen.

3

Convert to a job

Once they say yes, one click converts the quote into a scheduled job. No re-entering data. No confusion.

4

Track & complete

Mark jobs in progress, complete them when done, and watch your weekly revenue grow — all from a clean, simple dashboard.
